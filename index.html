Chemistry & Math LMS

An educational platform built with WordPress + Tutor LMS, designed to support high school students in learning Chemistry and Math. Features include interactive quizzes, periodic table games, bilingual content (Vietnamese/English), and AI-generated metadata for courses. The system aims to enhance self-learning with modern EdTech tools.
